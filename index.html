About me

Welcome to my home page !

I'm a research and teaching assisant at Le CNAM (Conservatoire National des Arts et Métiers) in Paris. Member of the Vertigo and ISID teams, which are databases/IS dedicated teams, component of the CEDRIC laboratory.

I hold a PhD Degree in Computer Science and
I'm specialized in social networks data management issues as well as recommender systems. If you have some massive datasets, let me know

  • Strong experience in data filtering, recommender systems and large scale data processing
  • MySQL, Oracle, MS SQL Server, Tomcat Server
  • Data visualization with d3js, Gephi, Tulip
